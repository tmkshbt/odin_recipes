Long Island Iced Tea

A boozy delight.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 fl oz vodka
  • 1/2 fl oz rum
  • 1/2 fl oz gin
  • 1/2 fl oz tequila
  • 1/2 fl oz triple sec
  • 1 fl oz sweet and sour mix
  • 1 fl oz coca cola
  • 1 lemon wedge

Directions:

  1. Mix liquors first.
  2. Then the sweet and sour ix and cola.
  3. Add the lemon wedge to the glass.
  4. Enjoy.
