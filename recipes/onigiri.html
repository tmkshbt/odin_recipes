Onigiri

A rice ball of surprise.

Prep: 20 min

Cook: 30 min

Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups uncooked short-grain white rice
  • 4 1/2 cups water
  • 2 sheets nori, cut into 1/2 inch strips
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • Filling of your choice (salmon, umeboshi, etc.)

Directions:

  1. Wash rice. Cook rice.
  2. Ball rice up and fill with your choice of filling.
  3. Gently press into a triangle. Cover with nori.
  4. Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
